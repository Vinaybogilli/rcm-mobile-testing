Our Services in Medical Billing

Patient Services

Our Patient Services team is dedicated to delivering a seamless, transparent experience for both patients and providers. We handle everything from verifying insurance coverage and estimating out-of-pocket costs to managing billing inquiries and secure payment collection. With expert support in RPM, CPM, and SPM services, we keep patients informed and practices efficient. Let us help you enhance patient satisfaction while easing your administrative workload.

Prior Authorizations

Our Prior Authorization team streamlines the entire authorization process to ensure timely care and reduce claim denials. From verifying requirements and submitting accurate requests to tracking statuses and managing denials, we handle every step with precision and compliance. We support providers with clinical documentation, proactive follow-ups, and effective resubmissions. Our goal is to accelerate approvals, minimize administrative burden, and keep your revenue cycle running smoothly.

Medical Coding

Our certified CPC medical coders deliver accurate, specialty-specific coding to accelerate reimbursements, reduce denials, and ensure audit readiness. We handle CPT, ICD-10, and HCPCS coding with strict adherence to payer guidelines and CMS rules. Through internal audits, denial trend analysis, and provider education, we maintain the highest coding standards. Our goal is to drive cleaner claims, compliance, and stronger revenue outcomes for your practice.

Charge Entry

Our Charge Entry team ensures precise and timely input of patient charges, laying the groundwork for clean claims and quicker reimbursements. We verify demographics, insurance details, and coding accuracy while flagging discrepancies and coordinating with the front office as needed. Specialized support for RPM, CPM, and SPM programs helps maintain compliance and timely submissions. Our focus is on reducing errors, boosting first-pass claim rates, and optimizing your revenue cycle.

AR Follow Up & Denial Management

Our AR Follow-up and Denial Management team drives timely reimbursements by proactively pursuing unpaid claims and resolving denials with accuracy and compliance. We identify root causes, resubmit corrected claims, and coordinate across teams to close documentation gaps and prevent future issues. From eligibility checks to appeal tracking, we ensure every claim gets the attention it deserves. Our goal is to reduce AR days, boost recovery rates, and strengthen your revenue cycle performance.

Payment Posting

Our Payment Posting team ensures accurate, timely posting of insurance and patient payments to support clear financial records and faster issue resolution. We verify payments against billed charges, flag discrepancies, and route denials for follow-up, ensuring no dollar is left behind. From daily reconciliation to supporting RPM, CPM, and SPM claims, we uphold posting compliance and transparency. Our goal is to enhance revenue visibility, reduce errors, and streamline your reimbursement process.
